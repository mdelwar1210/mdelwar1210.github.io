WellVerse

Project Overview: Wellverse is a mobile application that aims to revolutionize the way users engage with their smartwatches and fitness trackers. By combining immersive experiences, personalized routines, and community engagement, WellVerse empowers individuals to maintain their health and fitness goals, promoting continuous engagement and habit-building.


Team: Mayisha Delwar + Jessica Chiu


Prompt: How might we intentionally create a better world through the metaverse?


Problem Statement: 1/3 of smartwatch and fitness tracker users lose interest within the first six months, resulting in a breakdown of established fitness habits. Wellverse seeks to address this issue by creating a compelling and personalized health and fitness experience within the metaverse, aimed at reengaging users and fostering long-term engagement with their health and fitness goals.



UX Design Process:

  1. Empathize
  2. Define
  3. Ideate
  4. Prototype
  5. Test


Empathize


We initiated our research process by exploring overarching problem spaces and breaking them down into smaller, more specific problems that we could tackle through research and discussion. Some of the problem spaces we explored included the following:


  • Mental Health
  • Gaming
  • Housing
  • Traveling
  • Fitness

After researching each of these problem spaces, we made the decision to focus on the Fitness category and continued to further research common problems that occurred under the umbrella of Fitness and Technology. We created user personas to attempt to holistically understand the key groups that face problems and what those problems were.



Based on these user personas, we concluded that the design of WellVerse should aim to be fun and offer stress relief, encourage users to connect with their physical health, and provide a platform to build community within the fitness world. This insight guided our subsequent design decisions, ensuring that WellVerse was tailored to meet the specific needs and preferences of our target audience.


Research Process

Through extensive research, we delved into the behavior of smartwatch and fitness tracker users, their interests, and habits. Additionally, we explored the possibilities of the metaverse as a portal to immerse users in their health journey. The research provided valuable insights on user disengagement, the desire for immersive experiences, and the potential to leverage data from wearables. These findings informed the development of Wellverse, ensuring a user-centered design that addresses user needs and enhances engagement in the health and fitness realm.



Define


After getting more context on the users and the problem, it was now time to specifically define what problem we were trying to solve and who we were trying to solve it for. During this stage, we analyzed our research, defined our customer and user group, and honed in on a clear problem statement.


Target Audience

Our target audience comprises Generation Z/Young Adults and fitness tracker users. We chose these groups based on their relevance to the problem we identified and their potential to benefit from the solutions offered by Wellverse.


Some questions we asked to aid in creating our problem statement:


  • How might we use the metaverse to benefit in areas of mindfulness, mental health, and/or fitness?
  • How can we use the metaverse to track and improve the physical and mental health of young adults?
  • How might we use the metaverse to create communities amongst users who want to increase awareness with their health?
  • How might we use the metaverse to promote continuous health awareness in individuals who want to track their health?

After analyzing our research, we created an affinity map to help identify themes in overlapping ideas of fitness and the metaverse.




Ideate


Design Process

The design process for Wellverse involved thorough research, planning, and iterative development to ensure a user-centered and visually appealing experience. Key steps in the process included:


  • Thorough research to understand user needs and preferences.
  • Planning to establish project goals and objectives
  • Iterative development to refine the design based on user feedback.

Wireframing techniques were utilized to create a structured blueprint and a user flow diagram was created, guiding the layout and navigation design. User feedback and usability testing played a crucial role in iterating and refining the app's design.




Low Fidelity Wireframes




In addition to the design elements, visual branding was also a priority. A vision board featuring colors and inspiration was put together to guide the aesthetic direction of WellVerse.




Prototype


We began finalizing our ideas by creating hi-fidelity wireframes of our app, WellVerse, which we later enhanced with interactions to prototype our actions. Additionally, we established visual branding to ensure a cohesive and engaging user experience.


Visual Design

Wellverse incorporates visually appealing and intuitive design elements. The app features eye-catching images and an intuitive interface that facilitates easy access and seamless navigation. The color scheme and typography are carefully selected to enhance readability, user engagement, and a sense of harmony within the app's visual aesthetics.

Key Features and Solutions

  • Activity and Exercise Tracking: Wellverse collects data from smartwatches and fitness trackers, providing users with comprehensive insights into their physical activities and exercise routines.
  • Guided Meditation Exercises: The app offers a variety of mindfulness and meditation exercises, promoting mental well-being and stress reduction.
  • Fitness Activities: Wellverse provides a diverse range of fitness activities tailored to individual preferences and goals, offering an engaging and interactive workout experience.
  • Personalized Routines: Leveraging the collected data, Wellverse generates personalized routines and recommendations, motivating users to stay on track and achieve their health and fitness objectives.
  • Community Engagement: Wellverse fosters a supportive and connected community where users can join groups, share their progress, exchange tips, and support one another on their health and fitness journeys.
